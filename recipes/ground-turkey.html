Ground Turkey

Ingredients

  • 93% lean ground turkey
  • olive oil
  • creole seasoning
  • tomato paste
  • lime juice
  • tobasco sause
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Pour olive oil into a large pan on a stove

This is a very diverse dish that can be used in many foods like tacos, burritos, and lasagna. I have even mixed it in my mac and cheese.
