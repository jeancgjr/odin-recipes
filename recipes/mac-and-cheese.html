Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of shell pasta
  • ranch
  • carnation milk
  • shredded cheese
  • creole seasoning or whatever you prefer
  • lime juice

Steps

  1. Fill a deep pan with water to about 3/4ths of the way
  2. Put about 3 table spoons of lime juice in the water and creole seasing
  3. Mix and set on the stove on maximum heat and wait for it to boil
  4. Once the water is boiling, carefully pour the two boxes of shells into the water
  5. Let the shells boil for 10 minutes or until cooked, mixing every 2 minutes to ensure the shells do not stick to the bottom of the pan
  6. After 10 minutes of boiling, strain the shells and run cold water over them, otherwise, they will continue to cook and get mushy
  7. Warm up left over groud turkey on the stove on low heat. Skip this step if you do not want to add ground turkey.
  8. Add cheese, carnation milk, and ranch and mix until all ingrediants are evenly distributed
