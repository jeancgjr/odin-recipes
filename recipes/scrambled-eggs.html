Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • creole seasoning Yes, I like creole seasoning a lot
  • half an onion
  • olive oil
  • tobasco sause

Steps

  1. Dice half an onion and set to the side
  2. Crack three eggs and place yolks into a bowl
  3. Season yolk mix with creole seasoning and a little bit of tobasco until it has a uniform color
  4. Pour oil into pan and set to medium heat
  5. When the oil is hot enough, pour yolk mixture into pan
  6. pull the edges of the eggs towards the center when bubbles appear on the edges
  7. Flip the eggs until all parts are cooked!
